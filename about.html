At LOBA, we believe that the key to a brand's success is a well-rounded approach to brand building. By offering creative direction, design, and production all in one place, we ensure that every part of the brands we work with fits together seamlessly.

Whether it's a large-scale branding project—covering everything from identity and packaging to photography, campaigns, and digital content—or a smaller, one-off photoshoot or film, we approach every PROJECT with meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to craft.

We'd love to hear about your project and discuss how we can work together.

Creative Direction

For us creative direction is focused on positioning your brand as a leader at the creative forefront of your industry. We work closely with you, to develop a cohesive and compelling narrative that sets your brand apart and resonates with your audience. From concept to execution, we ensure every element aligns with your brand's identity, creating a seamless and impactful experience across all touch points. Let’s collaborate to craft a vision that establishes your brand as a true innovator.

Brand Strategy & Design

Our brand strategy and design services are dedicated to building a strong and authentic foundation for your brand. We dive deep into understanding your market, audience, and goals, crafting a strategy that not only defines who you are but also drives growth. Our design approach brings this strategy to life, creating visually striking and cohesive brand elements that capture your essence and set you apart.

Stills & Video Production

We manage every aspect of production, from assembling and coordinating a bespoke and talented crew to scouting the perfect locations. With our extensive network of skilled directors, photographers, stylists and talent, we ensure that every aspect is handled with expertise and precision. From concept to final output, we guide you through each stage, delivering stunning visuals that will elevate your brand.

Edit & Retouch

We have a talented team of retouchers, editors, colour graders, animators, 3D visualizers that take everything we do and breathe new life into the projects. We never settle for just ok, we’re always pushing boundaries to find new and interesting ways to edit, refine and retouch.
